Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Coffee Excellence

Founded in the year 2000, Brew&Bean Coffee Company emerged as the pioneer of coffee culture in India. What started as a single café with a vision to provide exceptional coffee experiences has grown into India's second-largest coffee chain.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that great coffee brings people together. We wanted to create spaces where communities could gather, conversations could flourish, and memories could be made over the perfect cup of coffee.

Today, with over 425 cafés across India, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Nepal, and Bangladesh, we continue to uphold our founding principles while embracing innovation and growth.

Our Journey

2000

The Beginning

Brew&Bean Coffee Company was founded with the vision to introduce premium coffee culture to India.

2005

First Expansion

Opened our first 10 cafés across major Indian cities, establishing our signature warm and welcoming atmosphere.

2010

International Growth

Expanded to Sri Lanka and Maldives, bringing our coffee culture to the international market.

2015

Regional Leader

2020

Digital Innovation

2025

Continued Excellence
